Actomorph body
Long body, long limbs, slender figure and thin bones.
Low fat percentage: This makes the body shape tight; This is due to the high metabolic rate and burning calories throughout the day.
Thin, not full muscles: Of course, the ectomorph gains muscle mass when adhering to proper training and diet, but it is not as full as other species.
Difficulty gaining weight: This type is considered the most difficult to gain weight, so it requires a high-calorie diet.
Mesomorph body
Sporty physique: This type tends to build muscle easily and with high physical fitness, with broad shoulders compared to the waist.
Average burning rate, easy fat loss when adhering to diet and exercise.
Strong and wide bones: This type has strong and wide bones that can withstand the intensity of exercise, while resisting injuries.
Endomorph body
Strong, developed muscles, such as those of the shoulders and legs, although they are not clearly defined as a result of the fat accumulated on them.
Wide skeleton.
Low burning rate: This reduces the speed of burning fat, and thus we find fat accumulated in the abdomen and buttocks area, which makes the body structure circular or oval.
